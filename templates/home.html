Outline







This is a real life implementation of the concept of page replacement in an operating system, where retailers will be making decisions on adding and removing products from their inventories following such algorithms. The efficiency of each inventory management strategy will be judged on the basis of “hits” & “misses” as per the page replacement algorithm.

Aim

The project aims at implementing various page replacement algorithms in a real life scenario.

  • First In First Out
  • Least Recently Used
  • Second Chance
  • Research based Algorithm


Objective



To run a simulation between retailers and customers and find the most efficient strategy for inventory management.
